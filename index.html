FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your journey with us.

What is the minimum booking distance?
We accept bookings for any distance — even local city transfers. There is no minimum distance requirement. Pricing is discussed based on trip details.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 3–5 days in advance for large group bookings. For urgent requirements, call us directly — we often accommodate last-minute bookings too.
Are tolls and driver allowance included?
Tolls, parking, and driver night allowance are discussed transparently at the time of quotation. We don't add hidden surprises — everything is disclosed before you confirm.
Can we hire for multi-day outstation trips?
Absolutely! Multi-day outstation packages are our specialty. Whether it's a 3-day pilgrimage or a week-long tour, we provide the vehicle and driver for the entire duration.
Do you provide vehicles for weddings?
Yes! We have a dedicated Wedding Special package with decorated coaches, uniformed drivers, and full coordination for baraat, guest transfers, and event logistics.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellations made more than 48 hours before departure receive a full refund. Within 48 hours, a partial charge may apply. Please call us to discuss — we are always flexible and understanding.
